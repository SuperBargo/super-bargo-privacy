Privacy Policy

Super Bargo

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Super Bargo ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Super Bargo:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number provided during registration.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, search queries, requests submitted, and deals made.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Location Data: Approximate location to show nearby shops (only when you grant permission).
  • Camera Access: To allow you to capture photos of products for bargaining requests (only when you choose to use this feature).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services.
  • Connect you with nearby shops and service providers.
  • Send OTP for secure login and verification.
  • Improve and personalize your experience.
  • Send important notifications about your requests and deals.
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with:

  • Shops and service providers (only the details necessary to fulfill your requests).
  • Service providers who help us operate the App (e.g., Supabase for data storage, SMS providers for OTP).
  • Law enforcement when required by applicable law.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption on Supabase servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt-out: Disable location sharing or notifications through your device settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After account deletion, we may retain anonymized data for analytics purposes. Transaction records may be kept as required by law.

7. Third-Party Services

The App may contain links to third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these services. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any information.

8. Children's Privacy

Super Bargo is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the App. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
